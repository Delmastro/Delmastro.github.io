👋 University Life & background

I am currently pursuing a Bachelor of Computer Science at the University of Queensland. Through my experience at university, I have developed interests in data science, cryptography and pattern recognition. I also find algorithms and their applications, in particular, very fascinating. And of course, generating web applications and creating experiences that resonant with people is what I am all about. I enjoy developing new tools that make my life more convenient and efficient.

🎹 Music and Improvisation

In my spare time, you will probably find me sitting back, strumming on a guitar. I enjoy playing and creating my own music. Jazz fusion is my favourite genre, however, I also like listening and playing along to classical composers. I am honoured to say, that I played alongside James Morrison at HOTA on the Gold Coast in 2017 and played in the Queensland Young Conservatorium. I am inspired by many musicians and would like to connect my passion of music with computer science.
